Mission & Vision
My personal mission is to help starting developers with learning the very basics. I do not share the philosophy of "Learn X language and you're good!". My philosophy is to pick a language to learn the basic principles, documentation protocols and how to go from zero to MVP to a deployed application.
On social media I see a plethora of influencers trying to push their programs and courses for specific languages with the promises of 6 figure jobs and high equity, when that is not the reason to start the course of a developer.
The future vision of having talented developers on the market is what we should all strive for and if I am the one to start this movement then I gladly will be at the forefront of it!
About me
I am a software developer (engineer, developer, rockstar) who has specialized in learning multiple languages and frameworks. I believe in "the right tool for the job". I am not a "front end" or "back end" developer, nor do I consider myself a "full stack" developer. I am a software developer.
As a colourblind developer I also try to immerse myself in the UX design world to be able to create applications and user interfaces for all to use that are easily accessible.
I have experience in different branches and sectors, ranging from CRM's to dashboard and portals to factory software to the energy market in the Netherlands. I do not call myself an expert in these branches, but more a flexible worker to learn the necessary ideals to create great software.
